I created this website to show my support to the skate community mainly here in Laguna.

And since I can't skate much these days (pun intended), I figured why not make a website right? So here you are visiting this stupid ass website.

I currently don't have future plans for what I will be posting on this website and what materials and content I will be posting here but this website will mostly focus on skate vids, images, ideas, insight and other stuff relating to skateboarding.
